Collocations

Types of collocations

verb + noun (verbo + sustantivo)

verb + particle (verbo + partícula/preposición)

adverb + adjective (adverbio + adjetivo)

adjective + noun (adjetivo + sustantivo)

Meaining of each term

noun = name

verb = action

adverb = frequently
