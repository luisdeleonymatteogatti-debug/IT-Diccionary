Email

Email structure

Steps in order to send an email

1) Attach documents or photos

2) Write the message and check spelling

3) Write the subject

4) Write the sender's address

5) Write the receiver's address

6) Send email

Structure of an informal email

Paragraph 1) Mention previous email and something

related to the receiver (family, problem, news)

Paragraph 2) You answer the previous message question

Paragraph 3) Give some news

Paragraph 4) Give an excuse to stop writing

Example of an informal email

From: m.tteo.gatti@gmail.com

To: teacherpunciolo@gmail.com

Subject: Breaking the iceee!!!

Dear Lorena,

Hi, how are you? Is everything ok, how was that presentation you were worried about?

Anyways, you said that Italian students are visiting your school and you want ideas to communicate with them and also with breaking the ice. Remember that most Italian words come from Latin (your favourite subject) so I think that if you try your best to speak Italian, they will understand. Also remember that some speak English but if that doesn't work you can also smile and use sign language, that will help with breaking the ice.

Remember that swimming competition that I told you about? Well, guess who won second place.

Well the break is over, gotta go to class. We should meet some day, goodbye.

Love

Matteo
