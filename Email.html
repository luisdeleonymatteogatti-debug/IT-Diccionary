Email

Email structure

Steps in order to send an email

1) Attach documents or photos

2) Write the message and check spelling

3) Write the subject

4) Write the sender's address

5) Write the receiver's address

6) Send email

Structure of an informal email

Paragraph 1) Mention previous email and something related to the receiver (family, problem, news)

Paragraph 2) You answer the previous message question

Paragraph 3) Give some news

Paragraph 4) Give an excuse to stop writing
